## Wording to avoid

When writing your own copy or modifying these templates, please **avoid** the following:

- **Asking for stars:** Do not add "Star this repo" or "Please give us a star".
- **Overclaiming capabilities:** Do not imply that the AI runs the entire project autonomously without human oversight.
- **Saying AI did everything:** Maintain the distinction between AI generation and human review/architecture.
- **Presenting Jules as a human contributor:** Jules must always be framed as an AI coding agent or managed tool.
- **Hype-heavy language:** Avoid words like "revolutionary", "game-changing", or "AGI". Keep it practical.
- **External rankings:** Do not mention trending lists, external leaderboards, or rankings.
