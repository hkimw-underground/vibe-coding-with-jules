# Public Share Kit

This kit provides reusable copy for sharing the **AI Coding Workflow Starter Kit for Jules**. Use these templates for GitHub profiles, social media, and community discussions to help others discover a structured, GitHub-native way to work with AI agents.

## Positioning Principles

- **Process Over Code**: Focus on how Jules works within a standard GitHub workflow.
- **Human-Led**: Emphasize that human maintainers own the architecture, review, and merge decisions.
- **Agentic Helper**: Frame Jules as an AI coding agent, not a human contributor or a replacement for engineering judgment.
- **Practical & Reusable**: Highlight the templates and examples that can be copied into any repository.

## Wording to Avoid

To maintain professional engineering standards and avoid hype, **do not use** the following framing:

- ❌ "AI that writes your entire project for you." (Overclaiming)
- ❌ "Our human-like AI developer Jules..." (Presenting Jules as human)
- ❌ "Stop coding and let AI take over." (Hype-heavy/Anti-human)
- ❌ "Please star this repository!" (Star-seeking)
- ❌ "Ranked #1 on [External List]." (External rankings)
- ❌ "The end of human programming." (Overclaiming/Hype)
